Local Learning Journey -Himachal

In Collaboration with Awakening Learning Center, Bir

DETAILS:
Dates: June 7th - June 11th (5 days/4 nights)
Age Group: Open for all
Destination: Bir Billimg, Palampur, Himachal Pradesh

WHAT TO EXPECT?
A journey which will take you to the parts that has gone missing in the complexity of the world today. Yet that which our soul desires for everyday. It will include:

  • Going back to connecting with our soil, roots and food
  • To understanding the wisdom behind simplicity, culture and joy that it brings
  • To capture the exquisite taste of local cuisine and the flavors that brings health along
  • Known for their spiritual wisdom monasteries will be a stop on our way
  • Touching the roots through art and theatre through stories
  • Witnessing the possibilities of living a different life. Meeting realities that are different then ours and seeking how we can attain those too

    • Sharing stay in a comfortable 2 story house with its own space for farming

    • Local, healthy meals

    • Local transportation

    • Cost for visiting, connecting to different communities and places
    Anything not a part of the above inclusions and any extra expenses are not included

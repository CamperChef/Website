Summer Camp

DETAILS:
Age Group: Kids below 16 Years
Destination: 70 Acre Organic Farm by a river, Karjat

WHAT TO EXPECT?
We are going to experience 3 days of adventure and learning in the widespread farm. It will include:

  • Nature Trail and Birds watching
  • Fun activities in the river and pool
  • Learning about Organic farming, other farm activities and trees
  • Outdoor and indoor Games
  • Movie night under the star lit sky
  • Community activities to learn from and with each other
  • The games will embibe confidence and the spirit of oneness along with a lot of fun
  • The games are also designed in a way that will involve a lot of senses together. Improving focus and coordination along with team work
  • Mulberry and Raw Mango harvesting
  • Art and making a handmade gift from nature to take back
  • Introduction to sustainability through a fun DIY best out of waste product making(Bring an old/discarded T shirt)
The 3 days will be full of experiences that will be close to nature and that will bring the kid close to themselves and others around. Forging strong bonds and creating lasting memories.

CONTRIBUTION DETAILS:
A contribution of 10000/- INR per person including:

  • Pickup and drop from Pune/Dadar Railway Station

  • Sharing stay in a comfortable room/tent

  • Completely Organic, healthy and wholesome food(4 meals a day)

  • Facilitation and activities

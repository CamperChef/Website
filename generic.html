Summer Camp

DETAILS:
Age Group: Kids below 16 Years
Destination: 70 Acre Organic Farm by a river, Karjat

WHAT TO EXPECT?
We are going to experience 3 days of adventure in the widespread farm. Not only will the fun revolve around learning activities in farm but also in the river that can we enjoyed with a view and enjoying in it. We will pluck fresh mulberiies and enjoy them. The kids will get involved in a lot of community learning and games. The games will be such that will embibe confidence and the spirit of oneness and fun in the kids. Not only that the kids will also learn a lot of new skills and take back with them amazing memories of 3 days of togetherness with each other and with nature all around.

CONTRIBUTION DETAILS:
A contribution of 10000/- INR per person including:
Pickup and drop from Pune and Dadar
Sharing stay in a comfortable room/tent
Organic and wholesome 4 times meals for 3 days
Facilitation and activities
